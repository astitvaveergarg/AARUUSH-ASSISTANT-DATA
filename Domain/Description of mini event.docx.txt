Description of mini event
Separate the ball 
                        Colored thermo Cole balls will be kept in a bowl. And the contestant has to pick the balls using a straw by sucking it and has to keep it in a separate bowl. Then the other contestant has to separate it according to the colors. 
Connect 4
It is a two player connection game in which the players first choose a color and then take turns dropping the colored discs from the top into a seven-column, six row-vertically suspended grid. The pieces fall straight down, occupying the next level space within the column. The objective of the game is to connect four of one’s own discs of the same color next to each other vertically, horizontally, or diagonally before your opponent.
