FUNDROID
This event is about playing the games one play in Android smart phone in real life.


LAN PARTY
This event is all about Computer Games which are to be played in team or without team on LAN like CS, FIFA, DOTA and NFS.


TECHIES 4.0
This event is based on a reality show named Roadies. It consists of both physical and mental tasks including vote outs and eliminations.


THE KNIGHT HUNT
This is Treasure Hunt, with the theme based on the legend The Dark Knight which is implemented very neatly in the event.


UV CRICKET
This is the special event in which the cricket will be played indoors in a dark room with UV paint on the stumps, cricket bat, tennis ball and the gloves. The crease, sign boards and other details will also be highlighted with the UV paint.
