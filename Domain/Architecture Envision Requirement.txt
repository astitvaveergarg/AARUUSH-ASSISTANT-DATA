What is your committee/domain/team about?
Architecture encompasses elements related to planning, design, usage of light, space, layouts, computer modelling software and programming that help digitalize architecture’s core processes, thereby providing a comprehensive experience of modern architecture. What you need is planning, innovation, efficiency and enhancing the designs and magic ensues.

Work profile 
Architecture is the art and technique of designing and building, as distinguished from the skills associated with construction.
